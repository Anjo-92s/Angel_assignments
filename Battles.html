Historical Battles

  • Battle of Thermopylae

    Date: 480 BC

    The Battle of Thermopylae was fought between an alliance of Greek city-states, led by King Leonidas I of Sparta, and the Persian Empire, led by King Xerxes I. Despite being vastly outnumbered, the Greeks held off the Persian army for several days before being defeated.

  • Battle of Hastings

    Date: October 14, 1066

    The Battle of Hastings was a pivotal event during the Norman conquest of England. It took place between the forces of William, Duke of Normandy, and King Harold II of England. William's victory led to the Norman control of England and had a significant impact on English history.

  • Battle of Waterloo

  • Battle of Gettysburg

    Date: July 1-3, 1863

    The Battle of Gettysburg was a significant engagement during the American Civil War. It was fought between the Confederate Army of Northern Virginia, commanded by General Robert E. Lee, and the Union Army of the Potomac, led by General George G. Meade. The Union's victory at Gettysburg is often considered a turning point in the war.

  • Battle of Stalingrad

    Date: August 23, 1942 - February 2, 1943

    The Battle of Stalingrad was a major confrontation between German and Soviet forces during World War II. It was fought for control of the city of Stalingrad (now Volgograd) in southwestern Russia. The Soviet Union's victory at Stalingrad marked a turning point in the war and is considered one of the bloodiest battles in history.
