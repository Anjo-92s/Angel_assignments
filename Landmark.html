List of Landmarks

  1. Eiffel Tower

    Location: Paris, France

    Description: The Eiffel Tower is an iconic iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris. It is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world and a symbol of France.

  2. Taj Mahal

    Location: Agra, India

    Description: The Taj Mahal is a magnificent white marble mausoleum situated on the southern bank of the Yamuna River in Agra. It was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife.

  3. Great Wall of China

    Location: China

    Description: The Great Wall of China is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most famous structures ever built. It stretches across the northern part of China, spanning thousands of miles.

  4. Statue of Liberty

    Location: New York, United States

    Description: The Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture located on Liberty Island in the New York Harbor. It was a gift from France to the United States and has become a symbol of freedom and democracy.

  5. Pyramids of Giza

    Location: Cairo, Egypt

    Description: The Pyramids of Giza are ancient structures located on the outskirts of Cairo. They were built as tombs for the pharaohs and are the only remaining wonders of the ancient world.
